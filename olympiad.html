About the Olympiad

More than an exam: a head start

The ALLIN Olympiad challenges students to think, not just memorise. By testing core reasoning in Mathematics and Science against peers across the country, it gives every young learner an honest benchmark of where they stand, and the motivation to climb higher.

For families, it's a low-pressure way to discover a child's strengths early. For students, it's the confidence that comes from competing on a national stage and being recognised for real ability.

How It Works

A simple, two-stage journey

Register, take Stage 1, and the top performers advance to the Stage 2 final.

1

Register

Sign up with a few basic details, choose your class and complete a simple registration, guided every step of the way.

2

Stage 1

A class-wise objective (MCQ) paper in Mathematics and Science, available in convenient online or offline modes.

3

Stage 2 Final

The top performers from Stage 1 advance to the final round that decides national ranks and award winners.

4

Results & Rewards

Receive a detailed performance report with your all-India rank and percentile and, for top scorers, prizes and scholarships.
