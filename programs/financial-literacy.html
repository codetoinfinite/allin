SSP Program · Grades 6–12

Financial Literacy

Money skills for life. Saving, budgeting and smart decisions: the financial common sense schools rarely teach, taught at exactly the right level for each grade.

About the program

Most people learn about money the hard way. This SSP track changes that: students learn how money works, why saving and budgeting matter, the basics of banking, interest and smart spending, and how to avoid common financial traps, all in age-appropriate language.

Older students go further: an introduction to investing concepts, goal-based planning and the link between careers and finances. As a finance-first institute, this is content we care about deeply, and it pairs naturally with our College and Skill finance courses.

Who is it for

  • Grade 6–12 students of every stream
  • Students who want practical, real-world money skills
  • Parents who value financial common sense early

What you'll gain

  • How money, saving and budgeting really work
  • Banking, interest and smart-spending basics
  • Avoiding common financial traps and scams
  • Intro to investing & goal-based planning (senior grades)
  • Confidence to make everyday money decisions
