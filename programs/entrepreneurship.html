Entrepreneurship

Turn ideas into ventures. A hands-on introduction to business thinking, problem-solving and building something real, long before college.

About the program

Entrepreneurship is a skill, and it can be taught early. This SSP track walks students through spotting problems worth solving, shaping an idea, understanding customers, basic money and pricing, and pitching with confidence, all in plain, practical language.

The centrepiece is a mini-startup project: students take an idea from sketch to a simple working plan or prototype and present it. They leave with a builder's mindset: curiosity, ownership and the courage to try.

Who is it for

  • Grade 6–12 students with ideas and curiosity
  • Future founders, creators and problem-solvers
  • Students who learn best by building, not memorising

What you'll gain

  • Business thinking & opportunity-spotting
  • Customer, pricing and basic-finance fundamentals
  • A hands-on mini-startup project
  • Pitching & presentation confidence
  • An ownership mindset that helps in any career
