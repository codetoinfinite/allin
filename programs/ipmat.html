IPMAT

Skip ahead to an IIM. IPMAT preparation that sharpens quantitative ability, verbal skills and logical reasoning for the five-year integrated management programme.

About the program

IPMAT opens the door to the IIMs' five-year Integrated Programme in Management, straight after grade 12. SSP builds the three pillars the exam tests: quantitative ability (both MCQ and short-answer), verbal ability, and logical reasoning.

Training mixes concept-building with timed sectional drills and full mocks, plus interview and written-ability preparation for the later selection rounds. Students learn to solve fast, write clearly, and present themselves with confidence.

Who is it for

  • Grade 11 & 12 students aiming for IIM Indore/Rohtak IPM
  • Strong quant-and-verbal students wanting an early B-school path
  • Anyone targeting top management programmes after class 12

What you'll gain

  • Quantitative ability: MCQ and short answer
  • Verbal ability & reading comprehension
  • Logical reasoning & data interpretation
  • Timed sectional drills and full mocks
  • WAT & personal-interview preparation
