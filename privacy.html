Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

Last updated: 9 June 2026

ALLIN ("ALLIN", "we", "us" or "our") operates this website and provides educational programs, courses and counselling services for students, parents and institutions across India. This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect through our website, enquiry forms, counselling calls and program enrolments.

1. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to respond to you and deliver our programs:

  • Details you give us: name, email address, phone number, class or grade, city and the message you share when you fill an enquiry form or book a counselling call.
  • Program information: the courses, exams or pathways you are interested in, and details relevant to your enrolment.
  • Usage information: basic, non-identifying data such as pages visited and device type, used to improve the website experience.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and arrange counselling calls.
  • Recommend the right program or pathway for your goals.
  • Provide course access, updates and learning support after enrolment.
  • Send important service updates and, where you have agreed, relevant program information.
  • Improve our website, content and services.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with trusted service providers who help us run our operations, such as form delivery, email and communication tools, and only to the extent needed to provide our services. We may also disclose information where required by law.

4. Cookies & preferences

Our website uses minimal browser storage to remember your preferences, such as your light or dark theme choice. You can clear this at any time through your browser settings.

5. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. While no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, we work to safeguard the data you share with us.

6. Students & parents

Many of our programs support school students. Where a student is a minor, we expect enquiries and enrolments to be made by a parent or guardian. We collect only the information necessary to deliver the program and respond to the family.

7. Your choices & rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop sending you program updates. To make a request, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Third-party links

Our website may link to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with the revised date.

10. Contact us

For any question about this policy or your information, write to us at anantgupta@gmail.com or get in touch through our contact page.
