About the Course

An Enrolled Agent (EA) is federally authorised by the US Internal Revenue Service to represent taxpayers, the only credential of its kind that is unrestricted across all 50 states. Our Tax Accounting Professional (TAP) track wraps practical US tax-prep skills around the EA syllabus, so you finish both certified and employable.

India has become a global hub for US tax and accounting work. EA-qualified professionals are in high demand at global capability centres and outsourcing firms. We pair the syllabus with A3 mentorship, hands-on software practice and a placement cell focused on US-tax roles.

Who Is It For?

  • Commerce students & graduates wanting a fast global credential
  • Professionals targeting US-tax roles in GCCs & KPOs
  • CA/ACCA aspirants who want an added taxation specialisation
