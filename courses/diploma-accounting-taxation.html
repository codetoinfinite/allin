About the Course

Every business, from a local shop to a large company, needs people who can keep the books clean, file taxes on time and report numbers that leaders can trust. This diploma is designed to make you exactly that person. Instead of theory alone, you work on real vouchers, real returns and real spreadsheets, so the skills stick.

The program blends concept clarity (the K in KICN) with daily hands-on practice, mentor-led confidence building and placement support. You learn through live and recorded classes you can revisit anytime, with structured assessments at every stage.

Who Is It For?

  • 12th-pass and commerce graduates who want a quick path to a finance job
  • CA/CMA aspirants who want practical, tool-based skills alongside their studies
  • Small-business owners and freelancers who manage their own accounts & taxes

Curriculum Breakdown

  • Double-entry & the accounting cycle
  • Journals, ledgers & trial balance
  • Finalisation: P&L and balance sheet
  • Reading & interpreting financial statements
  • Tally Prime: company, masters, vouchers, inventory
  • GST-enabled accounting in Tally
  • Excel: formulas, lookups & data cleanup
  • Pivot tables & MIS dashboards
  • GST: registration, invoicing & returns (GSTR-1/3B)
  • Input-tax credit & reconciliation
  • Income tax: heads, deductions & ITR filing
  • E-invoicing & compliance workflow
  • Salary structures, PF, ESI & TDS on salary
  • Payslip generation & statutory filings
  • MIS reports & variance analysis
  • Capstone: month-end close for a sample business

What You'll Be Able To Do

  • Maintain a company's books end-to-end in Tally Prime
  • File GST and income-tax returns accurately and on time
  • Run payroll and produce clean MIS reports for management
  • Step into roles like Accounts Executive, Tax Assistant or Accountant
