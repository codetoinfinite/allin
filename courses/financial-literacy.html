About the Course

Most people learn about money the hard way. This program flips that, giving you a clear, practical grounding in personal finance before mistakes become expensive. From your first salary to your first investment, you'll understand exactly what to do and why.

It's hands-on, jargon-free and relevant from day one. You'll build a real budget, understand how investments and compounding work, learn the basics of taxation, and walk away with habits that compound for the rest of your life.

Who Is It For?

  • Students & first-jobbers learning to manage money
  • Professionals wanting to invest with confidence
  • Founders & freelancers managing business and personal finances
