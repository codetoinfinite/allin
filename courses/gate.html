About the Exam

GATE is conducted once a year by IISc Bangalore and the seven older IITs on a rotational basis, on behalf of the National Coordination Board. It tests the comprehensive understanding of undergraduate engineering and science subjects. The GATE score is valid for three years and is used both for postgraduate admission and for direct recruitment into Public Sector Undertakings.

ALLIN's GATE prep is built on the KICN model — deep concept building, intensive problem-solving, General Aptitude and Engineering Mathematics mastery, and a graded test series that mirrors the real CBT, so you walk in with both knowledge and exam temperament.
