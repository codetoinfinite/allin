About the Course

The Certified Management Accountant (CMA) credential is built for finance professionals who want to drive decisions, not just record them. Where traditional accounting looks backward, the CMA looks forward, focusing on planning, analysis, control and strategy. Because it is awarded by the IMA and recognised across the globe, CMAs are sought after in multinationals, global capability centres (GCCs) and finance teams that value commercial insight.

Our programme pairs concept mastery (the K in KICN) with hands-on application on real business cases, mentor-led confidence building and a network that converts your qualification into a career. You learn through live and recorded classes you can revisit anytime, backed by structured testing at every stage.

Who Is It For?

  • Commerce students after 12th who want a global finance career
  • Graduates, MBAs, M.Coms and CA-Inter candidates seeking an international credential
  • Working professionals targeting FP&A, business finance and management roles
