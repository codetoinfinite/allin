About the Course

As companies operate and raise capital across borders, IFRS has become the dominant framework for financial reporting worldwide. A Diploma in IFRS proves you can prepare, interpret and reconcile financial statements under these standards, a skill in constant demand at MNCs, global capability centres and consulting firms.

Our programme is concise and practice-driven. You work through real recognition and measurement scenarios, learn the disclosure requirements, and master first-time adoption and consolidation, all with mentor support and a placement focus on reporting and consolidation roles.

Who Is It For?

  • CAs, ACCAs and CMAs adding a global reporting specialisation
  • Finance graduates with relevant work experience
  • Reporting, audit and consolidation professionals
