About the Exam

The UGC NET is held twice a year by the National Testing Agency on behalf of the University Grants Commission. Qualifying it is mandatory to become an Assistant Professor in Indian universities and colleges; a higher score additionally secures the Junior Research Fellowship and Ph.D. admission. For Commerce and Management postgraduates, it is the definitive gateway into academia and funded research.

ALLIN focuses where demand is highest — Commerce (accounting, finance, business economics, business law, marketing, management) and Management (HRM, organisational behaviour, business administration). We combine concept-first teaching with the often-decisive Paper 1 (Teaching & Research Aptitude) and a graded mock series.
