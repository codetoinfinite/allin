About ALLIN

All in global finance

ALLIN is India's premier institute for accounting and financing, built to connect Indian talent to worldwide finance careers through globally recognised credentials, real skills and genuine mentorship.

Our Story

Built by finance professionals, for ambitious learners

ALLIN began with a simple frustration: India produces brilliant, hard-working finance talent, yet too many students never discover the global pathways open to them (ACCA, CA, EA, IFRS) or the practical skills employers actually pay for. The classroom taught theory; the market wanted application.

We set out to close that gap. ALLIN brings together world-class credential preparation, hands-on skill training and mentorship under one roof, and extends it across the whole journey, from school students discovering their strengths to college graduates and professionals levelling up. The name says it: we go all in on every learner's success.

Our Mission

To make globally recognised finance education and career-defining skills accessible to every ambitious Indian learner, and to walk with them from first concept to first job and beyond.

Our Vision

A generation of Indian finance professionals competing and winning on the world stage: confident, credentialed, skilled and supported every step of the way.
